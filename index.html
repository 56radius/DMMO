About

DMMO is a vision the Lord gave us to turn men from darkness to light and from the power of Satan unto Christ. Our mandate to reach out to vulnerable pregnant women and children in the rural areas where civilization and modern medical treatment is far from the reach of the people.

Dignified Medical Mission Organization was established 24 November 2022. Our first outreach was to Garin Mallam Village of Lau Local Government Area of Taraba State, Nigeria. It was a glorious experience as the people were blessed both spiritually and health wise. As we had free checkups/investigation, free treatments and referrals where necessary.
